About Us

Hello everyone! I'm Blanca or Lala, to start this off I want to thank you for taking your time browsing through my shop and if you are interested in any of the products you can reach out to me by checking out Contact Us.

Gardening has been a hobby of mine ever since early 2000s and my passion for this hobby grew to the point that we have gardens inside and outside our home where all kinds of flora are being personally taken care of by my husband and myself. Due to the abundance of several kinds of flora inside our beloved home, we decided that it'd be best if we sold some of them so that we can earn extra money to expand our garden. But as of right now, due to time constraints regarding our recent schedules this January 2023 we're only able to take photos of only a few of what we have. On the bright side, once time constraints become less concerning we will be able to update the website if we wish to.
